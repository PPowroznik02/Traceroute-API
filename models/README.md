## Models

[Car delivery](car_delivery.md) model is dedicated for employees of courier companies and for people involved in the delivery.


[Hiking](hiking.md) model was created for nature lovers who would like to spend more time among green areas.


[Inline skates](inline_skates.md) model contains restrictions improving movement on roller skates in various urban areas.

[Wheelchair](wheelchair.md) model was created for the disabled peoples, it includes possible limitations in movement that may encounter such people.
